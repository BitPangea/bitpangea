The World existed.

Its parts had been distinguished.

Their relations had been revealed.

Yet existence was not emptiness.

Something was present.

Structure described what the World was.

Relation described how its parts belonged.

Neither answered what might exist within it.

The next question

What exists within the World?

The World did not answer all at once.

It waited to be revealed.
