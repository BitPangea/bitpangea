The Constitutional Home of World Property Inquiry

The World Properties Domain preserves the constitutional recognition of objective properties already possessed by The World. Its Codices do not create, grant, activate, or assemble those properties. They recognize and preserve what constitutional inquiry reveals.

Constitutional Question
What properties does the World possess?
Domain Doctrine The World possesses properties before those properties are discovered.

Constitutional Purpose

The World Properties Domain exists to preserve objective properties of the already-existing World.

A World Property may remain unknown, unnamed, unmapped, or unrecorded without being absent. Constitutional discovery recognizes the property; it does not bring the property into existence.

Mode of Discovery

World Property Codices operate through ontological independence. No property must be constitutionally created or activated by another.

Their ordering reflects constitutional priority of recognition: the principled order through which properties are most faithfully preserved.

Each class of World Properties follows its own natural mode of revelation.

World Property Categories

World Properties may be organized into constitutional categories according to the nature of the properties they preserve.

  • Categories are classifications within the World Properties Domain.
  • Categories are not additional Domains.
  • Categories emerge through constitutional discovery.
  • No category is presumed before its constitutional recognition.
  • Classification follows what The World reveals.

Future Constitutional Inquiry

The World Properties Domain remains open to further discovery.

Future Codices may recognize additional objective properties. Future inquiry may also reveal additional constitutional categories through which related properties can be faithfully organized.

Neither the number of World Properties nor the number of categories is predetermined.

Constitutional Conclusion The Codex does not decide what properties The World should possess. It preserves what The World reveals that it already possesses.
