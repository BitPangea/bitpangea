The World Properties Domain preserves objective properties already existing within The World. The entries below reflect the current constitutional record of recognized World Properties.

WP-I · Extent

The cartographic property describing the spatial scope of the World.

WP-II · Region

The cartographic property describing a coherent large-scale portion of the World.

WP-III · Cluster

The cartographic property describing a contiguous portion of the World.

World Property Codices operate under ontological independence. Their order reflects constitutional priority of recognition and the natural mode of revelation, not logical dependency or creation.
