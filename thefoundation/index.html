The Constitutional Library of BitPangea

The Foundation preserves the constitutional and philosophical works upon which BitPangea is established. Together, these works define the existence, purpose, structure, language, truths, principles, wisdom, and preserved memory of the world.

Foundational Purpose The Foundation is not a single document. It is the constitutional library of the world: the shelf upon which BitPangea preserves the works that make the world intelligible.

The Foundational Works

Each work within The Foundation serves a distinct constitutional role. The Declaration proclaims the world. The Charter preserves its purpose. The Codex defines its structure. The Lexicon orders its language. The Truths state what is unconditionally true. The Principles guide stewardship. The Maxims preserve enduring wisdom. The Archive remembers what has been preserved.

These works do not replace one another. They form a cumulative foundation for understanding what BitPangea is, how it is known, and why it remains.
